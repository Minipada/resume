About me

I am an engineer working full time in Germany with industrial robots for years. I provide tools for the development team and shape the CI/CD pipeline. I love automation, enabling teams to deliver faster code. Tools only are not what makes devops succeed well. Collaboration, affinity and scaling are also essential.

Beside devops, I have been working in the past with high and low level robotics (ROS and microcontroller programming). I still have much interest in opensource projects like ROS and Yocto to which I often contribute.
