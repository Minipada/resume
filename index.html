About me

I am a devops engineer working full time in Germany with industrial robots for years and now providing devops services as a freelance on top of it. I provide tools for the development team and shape the CI/CD pipeline. I love automation, enabling teams to deliver faster code by doing better continuous integration.

Beside devops, I have been working in the past with high and low level robotics (ROS and microcontroller programming). I still have much interest in opensource projects like ROS, Yocto and Linux kernel to which I often contribute.
